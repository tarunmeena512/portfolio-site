Welcome to LNCPs, Indore

LN COLLEGE OF PHARMACEUTICAL STUDIES is an indispensable part of LNCT Group of Colleges, First Self Financing Engineering College of North Central India.

LN COLLEGE OF PHARMACEUTICAL STUDIES , Indore established in 2022 , renowned as LNCPs college indore for imparting the best quality of pharmaceutical education to create technical man power for special needs of the industry at both home and abroad.

LN COLLEGE OF PHARMACEUTICAL STUDIES (LNCPs COLLEGE) Indore, established in 2022, is a private institution under the Rishiraj Singh Memorial Welfare Society. It's located along the RAU-Pithampur Road, A Head IIM, Gram Bheslay, Indore and is affiliated with RGPV Bhopal. LNCP is approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and the (AICTE). The college offer undergraduate (B.Pharm) and Diploma (D.Pharm) programs in pharmacy, aiming to produce skilled graduates for the pharmaceutical industry.
